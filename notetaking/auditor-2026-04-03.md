# UI/UX Audit — AkademiWeal
**Date:** 2026-04-03
**Auditor Role:** Senior UI/UX Designer
**Scope:** Full frontend — all pages, components, design system

---

## Overall Score: 7.2 / 10

The learning side of the app is genuinely polished — close to 9/10. The Arena side was built fast and it shows — the bones are solid but the polish that makes the learning screens delightful hasn't carried over yet. The biggest wins are the bottom sheet trading flow and the chart tooltip, both of which will dramatically increase how "real" the trading simulator feels.

---

## ✅ What's Already Good

### 1. Design Language & Visual Identity
Strong, cohesive brand. Fredoka One for playful emphasis + Nunito for body creates a unique "edutainment" voice. The mascot system, gold/green/navy palette, and dark hero headers are premium-feeling and consistent across learning screens.

### 2. Gamification Layer
XP popups, star animations, mascot evolution, streak tracking, and sound effects are well-timed and non-intrusive. The `prefers-reduced-motion` support shows real craft.

### 3. Mobile-First Discipline
Fixed 448px max-width container, safe-area insets, 44×44px minimum touch targets on most buttons — solid mobile engineering.

### 4. Accessibility Foundation
`aria-label`, `role="status"`, `aria-live="polite"`, `role="dialog"` — better than most startup apps.

### 5. Lesson UX (Lesson.jsx)
The two-zone layout (illustrated scene + answer sheet) is the best screen in the app. Clear information hierarchy, color-coded options, animated feedback. This is polished.

### 6. GuestUnlock.jsx
The glassmorphic card + floating emoji + tappable coin is delightful. One of the strongest conversion screens in the app.

## ❌ What Needs to Change

---

### 🔴 Critical — Fix These First

> **Status as of 2026-04-03:** All 4 critical items resolved. ✅

#### 1. Two completely different styling systems — ✅ FIXED

Arena pages (ArenaStock, ArenaTrade, Arena) use 100% inline `style={{}}` objects. All other pages use Tailwind. This creates:
- Colors hardcoded as `#4ADE80`, `#0A2010`, `#F87171` with no central token
- If you want to update the brand green, you touch 30+ files
- Visual drift will grow worse over time

**Fix:** Extract Arena-specific values into CSS variables in `index.css`:
```css
:root {
  --arena-bg: #0A2010;
  --arena-accent: #4ADE80;
  --arena-sell: #F87171;
}
```

---

#### 2. Font sizes below WCAG minimum — ✅ FIXED

Multiple screens use 9px and 10px text:
- Y-axis chart labels: `fontSize: 9`
- OHLC column labels: `fontSize: 9`
- Speed hint text: `fontSize: 9`
- Time axis labels: `fontSize: 9`
- Order history rows: `fontSize: 10`

WCAG 2.1 AA minimum is **12px** for body text. On older Android phones with lower DPI, 9px is physically unreadable.

**Fix:** Minimum 11px for labels, 12px for any content the user needs to act on.

---

#### 3. Touch targets too small in simulation controls — ✅ FIXED

The speed buttons `1× / 5× / 10× / 100×` use `padding: '6px 8px'` — approximately 30px tall. Apple HIG and Material Design both require **44px minimum** touch target.

During simulation, users are likely tapping these while watching the chart — small targets cause mis-taps.

**Fix:** Minimum `height: 44px` on all interactive elements.

---

#### 4. No skeleton loaders on Arena pages — ✅ FIXED

Feed.jsx shows 3 skeleton cards while loading. Arena, ArenaStock, and ArenaTrade show nothing or just "memuat..." text. The visual jump when data loads is jarring.

**Fix:** Add skeleton cards matching the real layout shape for Arena, ArenaStock holding card, and chart area.

---

### 🟠 High Priority — Significantly Improves UX

#### 5. Back navigation is inconsistent — ✅ FIXED (tab bar removes need for back from Arena)

| Screen | Back button label |
|--------|-------------------|
| ArenaTrade | "← Kembali ke Chart" |
| ArenaStock | "← Arena" |
| StockDetail | "← Kembali ke Feed" |
| Lesson | ✕ icon (no label) |

Users build mental models from patterns. Pick one: either always use `←` with the screen name, or always use `navigate(-1)` with a generic label.

**Fix:** Standardize to `← [Parent Screen Name]` throughout.

---

#### 6. Arena.jsx is information overloaded — ✅ FIXED

Five sections visible on one scroll: portfolio stats → holdings → pending orders → market watchlist → order history. That's overwhelming, especially for new users.

**Fix:** Use a **tab bar** inside Arena:
- Tab 1: **Portofolio** (holdings + stats)
- Tab 2: **Pasar** (watchlist)
- Tab 3: **Riwayat** (order history + pending)

---

#### 7. ArenaTrade should be a bottom sheet, not a separate page — ✅ FIXED

The current flow: ArenaStock → navigate to `/arena/trade/:code` → full page reload → back to chart. This is 3 navigation events for one action.

Real trading apps (Ajaib, Stockbit) use a **bottom sheet** that slides up from ArenaStock itself. The chart stays visible in the background — users can see the price they're trading at.

**Fix:** Replace ArenaTrade page with a `<BottomSheet>` component inside ArenaStock.

---

#### 8. No interactive chart tooltip — ✅ FIXED

During simulation, users can see the chart line moving but can't tap a specific bar to see its OHLC values. The chart is 100% passive.

**Fix:** Add a tap/drag gesture on the chart that shows a vertical crosshair + tooltip with OHLC for the selected bar. This is the core interaction of every serious trading chart.

---

#### 9. OJK disclaimer design is alarming, not informative

Current: Bright yellow `⚠️` banner at top of ArenaTrade. It reads like an error or danger warning. New users will be put off before they've even made their first trade.

**Fix:** Redesign as a calm, educational callout — green or teal background, use an ℹ️ icon instead of ⚠️, softer language:
> ℹ️ *Ini adalah simulasi edukasi dengan uang virtual. Bukan rekomendasi investasi.*

Put it in the body of the form, not as a sticky banner at the top.

---

#### 10. Simulation progress bar is 6px tall and invisible

The `height: 6` progress bar during simulation is nearly impossible to see on a real device. It communicates no useful information at a glance.

**Fix:** Make it at least 10px, add the current bar time directly on the bar as an overlay label, and round the progress capsule to match the app's border-radius system.

---

### 🟡 Medium Priority — Polish

#### 11. Empty state screens are missing

| Situation | Current | Needed |
|-----------|---------|--------|
| No holdings yet | Gray card "📭 Kamu belum punya saham" | Illustration + CTA "Mulai beli saham pertamamu" |
| No pending orders | Section just disappears | "Tidak ada order aktif" with info |
| Chart no data | Hourglass + small text | Cleaner empty state with market hours info |
| Leaderboard loading failed | Text error | Illustrated error + retry button |

---

#### 12. Success screen after order lacks energy

After placing an order, the success screen is a plain card with ✅ or ⏳. Compare this to the Lesson result screen which has confetti, mascot animations, and star pops. The trading success moment should feel rewarding.

**Fix:** Add a brief confetti animation + mascot reaction on order filled. This reinforces the positive feedback loop.

---

#### 13. No visual indication of market hours — ✅ FIXED

Users don't know if the market is open or closed from the Arena screen. There's no badge or status indicator.

**Fix:** Add a small pill in the Arena header: `🟢 Pasar Buka · 09:00–15:00` or `🔴 Pasar Tutup` based on current WIB time.

---

#### 14. Holding card P&L color doesn't reflect state

When holding P&L is negative, the card background is still `#E8FFF0` (green). The card should reflect the P&L state:
- Profit → green tint (current)
- Loss → red/warm tint

---

#### 15. ArenaStock body section has no visual separation from the dark header

The transition from the dark green header to the white body is abrupt. The rounded top corners help but the jump is harsh.

**Fix:** Add a subtle gradient fade (`rgba(255,255,255,0)` to `white`) at the transition boundary, or use a card that overlaps the header slightly with a shadow.

---

### 🔵 UX Writing (Copy)

#### 16. Mix of formal and casual Indonesian

Some screens use formal Bahasa ("Menggunakan", "Mengambil"), others use casual slang ("Kamu udah 6 bulan di Weal!"). Pick one register and stick to it. For a youth edtech product, **casual but clear** is the right call throughout.

#### 17. "Simulasi Edukasi" label appears 3 times

Between ArenaStock and ArenaTrade, users see the OJK disclaimer twice before making a trade. Once is enough. Keep it only in ArenaTrade.
