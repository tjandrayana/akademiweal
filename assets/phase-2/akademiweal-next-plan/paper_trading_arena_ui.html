🏆 Leaderboard Design
3-tier league system (Gold/Silver/Bronze) — prevents beginners from feeling hopeless against veterans. Everyone competes in their league.
Countdown timer — creates urgency. Users who see "2 days left" will open the app to improve their rank.
Mascot visible on leaderboard — Vixen at #1 signals "Gold-tier mascots win". Junior users aspire to both rank AND mascot upgrade simultaneously.
"YOU" highlight — anchoring. Users immediately see themselves, which is more motivating than scrolling to find themselves.
💰 Monetization Hook
Free users can only see 5 stocks and trade in Bronze League. Premium = all 50 stocks + Gold League access + AI insights.
Season rewards — top 3 earn cosmetic mascot frames. Premium-exclusive frames available for purchase (Rp 15K each).
Share results — auto-generate IG story card showing rank + portfolio chart. Organic acquisition loop.
