P1
Foundation — Build the Arena
Core paper trading mechanics + free tier
Month 1–2
▼
P1-T01
Integrate IDX real-time price feed
Connect to Yahoo Finance API or IDX official data feed. Display live OHLCV for at least 50 IDX blue-chip stocks (LQ45 universe). Update interval: 5 minutes during market hours (09:00–15:00 WIB Mon-Fri).
DEV 🔴 CRITICAL Effort: 5d
P1-T02
Build virtual portfolio engine
Each user gets Rp 10.000.000 virtual capital on Monday 00:00 WIB. Track: holdings (lot qty, avg buy price, current value), cash balance, realized P&L, unrealized P&L, trade history. Reset every Monday. No carry-over.
DEV 🔴 CRITICAL Effort: 7d
P1-T03
Implement buy/sell trade execution flow
Market order only (no limit orders in v1 — reduces complexity). Validate: lot quantity ≥ 1, total cost ≤ cash balance, market is open. Show confirmation modal with order summary before executing. Success toast + XP animation on confirm.
DEV 🔴 CRITICAL Effort: 4d Deps: P1-T01, P1-T02
P1-T04
Weekly leaderboard computation
Calculate leaderboard by: (portfolio_value + cash_balance) / 10_000_000 - 1 = return_pct. Run compute job every 15 min during market hours. Snapshot final value Friday 15:30 WIB for season winner. Assign league: Top 10% = Gold, 10-30% = Silver, rest = Bronze.
DEV 🟠 HIGH Effort: 3d Deps: P1-T02
P1-T05
Arena UI implementation (4 screens)
Build all 4 screens per the design: (1) Arena Home with portfolio chart, (2) Stock Picker with search + filter, (3) Trade Execution with buy/sell toggle, (4) Leaderboard with 3-tier podium. Match InvestQuest v2 visual system.
DEV DESIGN 🔴 CRITICAL Effort: 8d
P1-T06
Mission system — link Arena to lesson XP
Define 5 weekly missions that bridge lessons to trading. Examples: "Beli saham perbankan" (+25 XP), "Miliki 3 sektor berbeda" (+50 XP), "Jual saham dengan profit" (+30 XP). Missions auto-reset weekly. Completing all 5 = +100 bonus XP.
DEV CONTENT 🟠 HIGH Effort: 3d
P1-T07
OJK compliance review — paper trading disclaimer
Consult with legal advisor to confirm paper trading (virtual money) does not require WPPE license. Add visible disclaimer on every trade screen: "Ini adalah simulasi. Uang virtual. Bukan rekomendasi investasi." Must be on-screen, not just in T&C.

⚠️ Risk Register
AI Agent must check this register before executing any task in the flagged phases. Log risk if triggered.
🔴 HIGH RISK
OJK Regulatory Shift
OJK issues new ruling that classifies paper trading summaries as investment advice requiring WPPE license.
Mitigation: Quarterly legal review. Maintain explicit "BUKAN REKOMENDASI" label on all stock content.
🔴 HIGH RISK
Data API Cost Spike
Yahoo Finance or data provider changes pricing/terms, increasing COGS significantly at scale.
Mitigation: P1 — evaluate IDX official API. Build abstraction layer so provider can be swapped.
🟠 MEDIUM RISK
High Churn Post-Premium
Users subscribe for 1 month to try, then cancel. MRR grows slowly despite high signups.
Mitigation: Annual plan discount (P3-T01), Stock School content lock (P4-T01), friend challenge retention (P4-T05).
🟠 MEDIUM RISK
Copycat Competitor
Stockbit or Bibit launches their own paper trading with gamification using their existing user base.
Mitigation: Moat = mascot evolution + educational curriculum, not just trading. Hard to replicate quickly.
🟢 LOW RISK
Market Crash Drops Engagement
IHSG crashes (as in April 2025 -15%) — users see losses and stop playing Arena.
Mitigation: Weekly reset = losses don't persist. Historical data shows IDX Arena activity increased during April 2025 volatility.
🟢 LOW RISK
Payment Friction (Indonesia)
Users want to subscribe but don't have credit cards. QRIS/GoPay penetration gap.
Mitigation: Midtrans supports QRIS, GoPay, OVO, VA — covers 95%+ of Indonesian digital payment methods.
